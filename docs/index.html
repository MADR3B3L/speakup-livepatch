Native macOS app for voice-powered text correction and keyboard shortcuts — without interrupting your flow.

I built this because I needed it.

When your hands aren't keeping up — from fatigue, repetitive strain, or anything that makes the keyboard harder than it should be — errors pile up faster than you can fix them. Switching to a mouse breaks flow. Dictation apps take over your screen and demand your full attention.

SpeakUp is what I wanted: something that fits around your work, not on top of it. Say the word, it patches in place, you keep going.

How it works

01/
Double-tap Right ⌘
Activates listening. A soft indicator lets you know it's ready. No mode switch, no window hijack.
02/
Say the word
Speak the correction, shortcut, or command. SpeakUp hears you on-device — nothing leaves your Mac.
03/
Done
The typo is patched in the active field, cursor restored. Or the shortcut fires. You never left your work.
🔒 100% on-device · no cloud

⌨️ works with any app
