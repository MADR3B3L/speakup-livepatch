Voice control that gets out of your way.

Say the fix. Keep typing.

I built this because I needed it.

Typing with certain conditions means errors stack up faster than you can catch them. Switching to a mouse breaks flow. Dictation apps take over your screen. None of it works when you're trying to actually stay in the work.

SpeakUp is what I wanted: a voice layer that fits around your work, not on top of it.

What it does

Fix while you type
Say the correction. SpeakUp finds the closest match in your focused field and patches it in-place — cursor restored, no interruption.
Control without leaving
paste undo action next tab media volume up — shortcuts fire without touching the keyboard.
Prepare without interrupting
mac prepare Gmail opens it in the background. mac work with Chrome snaps a split. Your hands stay where they are.

Something bigger is coming.

An adaptive layer that learns your specific patterns — fixing your particular errors, understanding your voice, getting faster over time. Free tier planned.
